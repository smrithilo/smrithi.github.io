Hello

I'm Smrithi and I'm pursuing my Master's in Robotics at the University of Pennsylvania. I'm trying to figure out my niche but I'm broadly working in multi-agent coordination and learning!

Before Penn, I did my bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering, with a minor in Robotics and Automation at BITS Pilani, India. I spent my senior year doing my bachelor's thesis at CMU's Robotics Institute under Prof Howie Choset and a semester-abroad at Mälardalens University, Sweden.

Outside research, I love taking photos on analog and digital cameras, paint, fly RC planes, and travel when I can —
